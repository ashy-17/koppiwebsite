Our Story

Founded in 2015, Brew & Bean began as a small coffee cart in Rizal.Laguna. Our founder, Princess Cristal, had a simple vision: to serve exceptional coffee that tells a story.

What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved local spot, known for our carefully sourced beans and artisanal brewing methods. Today, we continue to honor our roots while innovating for the future of coffee.

Every cup we serve reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the craft of coffee making. We partner directly with farmers who share our values, ensuring fair trade and environmentally responsible practices.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally friendly practices, from sourcing to serving. Our coffee is 100% sustainably grown and ethically sourced.

Quality

Every bean is carefully selected and roasted to perfection. We never compromise on quality, ensuring each cup meets our high standards.

Community

We believe coffee brings people together. Our shops are designed to be welcoming spaces where connections are made and ideas are shared.
